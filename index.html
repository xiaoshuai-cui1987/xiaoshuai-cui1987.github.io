About Us

Shaanxi Heavy Duty Automobile Import & Export Co., Ltd. is a professional company engaged in the export trade of Shaanxi Heavy Duty Automobile. The company has a professional team with rich experience and a perfect after-sales service system. Our products are sold to more than 60 countries and regions around the world, and are widely welcomed by customers.

Our main business includes: Shaanxi Heavy Duty Automobile (Shacman) dump trucks, tractor trucks, concrete mixer trucks, truck cranes, mining trucks and other special vehicles for export trade.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive pre-sales, in-sales and after-sales services to ensure that our customers get the best experience.

  • Pre-sales Service: Professional product consultation and selection guidance
  • In-sales Service: Timely production updates and quality control reports
  • After-sales Service: Technical support and spare parts supply worldwide
  • Customization: Special configurations to meet specific regional requirements
  • Training: Operator and maintenance training programs
